About Us

Commerce Academy was founded in 2010 with the mission of providing high-quality education to commerce students. Our goal is to help students excel in grades 11 and 12, and to prepare them for the CA Foundation exams.

We have a team of experienced and dedicated teachers who ensure every student gets the attention and guidance they need to succeed.
